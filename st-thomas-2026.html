Where We're Going

The Islands

St. Thomas is our home base. St. John is 20 minutes away by ferry. Virgin Gorda (BVI) is farther east — a longer boat ride but home to The Baths.

View larger map ↗
✈
St. Thomas (STT)
Home base. Charlotte Amalie, Magens Bay, Coki Beach. Great food, shopping, and nightlife. No passport needed.
🌿
St. John (USVI)
20-min ferry from Red Hook. ~60% national park. Trunk Bay, Cinnamon Bay, Lime Out floating restaurant. No passport.
🪨
Virgin Gorda (BVI)
The Baths: massive boulder grottos and sea caves. Longer boat ride (~1.5–2 hrs). Passport required + BVI customs.

Red Hook ferry (~$15–20/pp) → St. John taxi → Lime Out water taxi (~$30/pp) → taxi back → ferry back. Lots of transfers and per-person costs add up fast. Doable, but less seamless.

🚗 Rental car vs. taxi — rental wins for groups

  • No Uber or Lyft on St. Thomas — taxi association has a monopoly. Taxis charge per person, $10–30/pp each way.
  • A $100/day rental split among 4–5 people is just $20–25/person/day — far cheaper than taxis for island exploring.
  • Heads up: driving is on the left side of the road in USVI (steering wheel is still on the left though).
  • You can take your rental car on the St. John ferry — a big advantage over taxis for exploring the island.
